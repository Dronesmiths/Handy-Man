The Ultimate Home Improvement Checklist for Antelope Valley Residents

Your year-round guide to protecting your investment in the High Desert.

Owning a home in the Antelope Valley is unique. Between the 100-degree summers, 30-degree winters, and the legendary 60mph gusts, your property faces challenges that coastal homes never see.

Use this comprehensive checklist to stay ahead of the curve.

Spring

Preparation for the heat begins now.

  • HVAC Service: Tune up the AC before the first 90-degree day.
  • Irrigation Audit: Check for leaks from winter freezes.
  • Exterior Painting: Best time for touch-ups before the baking sun arrives.
  • Deck & Patio Seal: Protect wood and concrete from UV damage.

Summer

Focus on cooling and protection.

  • Window Seals: Keep the heat out and the bill down.
  • Attic Fan Check: Ensure ventilation is exhausting hot air.
  • Pool Prep: Regular maintenance for heavy usage.
  • Fire Clearance: Clear dry brush as required by local ordinance.

Fall

Preparing for the wind and rain.

  • Fence Bracing: Strengthen posts before winter wind storms.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clear desert dust before the rains.
  • Roof Inspection: Look for loose shingles or flashing gaps.
  • Tree Trimming: Remove dead wood that could fall on your house.

Winter

Indoor projects and freeze protection.

  • Pipe Insulation: Prevent bursts on freezing desert nights.
  • Drywall Repairs: Perfect time for interior cosmetic work.
  • Draft Defense: Replace door sweeps and weather stripping.
  • Smoke Alarms: Mandatory battery check and sensor cleaning.
